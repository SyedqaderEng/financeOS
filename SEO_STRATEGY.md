### FAQ Section

**Q1: Is my financial data secure?**
A: Absolutely. FinanceOS uses bank-level 256-bit encryption and read-only access to your accounts. We never store your banking passwords, and we're SOC 2 Type II certified. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, and we never sell your information to third parties.

**Q2: How does bank sync work?**
A: We partner with Plaid, the industry leader in secure bank connections. Plaid connects to 10,000+ financial institutions in the US and Canada. Your transactions are automatically imported daily, so your data is always up to date without manual entry.

**Q3: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes! There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings, and it will remain active until the end of your billing period.

**Q4: What makes FinanceOS different from Mint or YNAB?**
A: Unlike Mint (discontinued), FinanceOS is actively developed with modern features and security. Compared to YNAB, we offer automatic bank sync at a lower price point, plus investment tracking and multi-currency support that YNAB doesn't have.

**Q5: Do I need to manually enter transactions?**
A: No! FinanceOS automatically imports all transactions from connected accounts. You can manually add cash transactions if needed, but most users never have to enter anything.

**Q6: Can I share my budget with my spouse/partner?**
A: Yes! The Family plan supports up to 5 users with shared budgets and accounts. Perfect for couples managing money together or families tracking their finances.

**Q7: Does FinanceOS work on mobile?**
A: Yes! We have native iOS and Android apps that sync seamlessly with the web version. Access your finances anywhere, anytime.

**Q8: What if I have accounts in different currencies?**
A: The Plus and Family plans include multi-currency support with automatic exchange rate updates. Track accounts in USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, and 50+ other currencies.

**Q9: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes! Plus and Family plans include data export to CSV, Excel, and PDF formats. You own your data and can take it with you anytime.

**Q10: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! All plans include a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start.
